Attractiveness

It is located at the transportation hub in Asia. The distance between Taoyuan Airport and cities in Asia such as Tokyo, Singapore and Hong Kong averages about three hours away by plane.
  • Aviation Advantage
  • Strategic Location

Honors

Taoyuan has a variety of diverse industries as well as a complete industrial structure, which are good for nurturing the creation of various industrial chains. In the future, six major industries, including cloud computing, smart vehicles, international logistics, biotechnology, aviation support, and green energy, will be important foci for development.
